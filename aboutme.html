About me

My name is Luis, and I am a qualified Personal Trainer achieving Level 3 in a PT Diploma in 2008. I have been a personal trainer for over 10 years.

I am passionate about working with individuals to achieve their own individual goals. I can provide assistance with training plans and nutritional advice to ensure a fully balanced personal schedule is provided.

I am able to provide training within the Aqua Vale Swimming and Fitness centre in Aylesbury subject to availability. And for those that find gyms daunting, I am available to come to your home and offer a one on one service (provided you are in a 25Km distance of Aylesbury). Otherwise a suitable outdoor space nearby can be arranged, especially for larger groups.

If you would like to ask questions or find out more, please send me a message on the enquire form here or contact me via social media.

Thank you and I look forward to kick start your journey.
